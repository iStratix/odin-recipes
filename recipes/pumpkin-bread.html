Pumpkin Bread

Description

This is the best pumpkin bread recipe I have ever made. Once finished, the bread is incredibly moist and delicious and is my favorite autumn dessert! The bread utilizes a variety of fantastic fall spices which never fails to make my kitchen smell homely and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon allspice
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1 1/3 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Prep a 9x5 inch loaf pan by lining with parchment paper or butter and flour it.
  2. Sift together flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and spices in a large bowl then whisk together and set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl combine the oil, sugars and eggs then mix well. Add the pumpkin puree, milk, and vanilla. Whisk again until combined.
  4. Pour the wet mixture into the the dry and mix until just combined.
  5. Pour batter into your prepared 9x5 inch pan.
  6. Bake for about an hour at 350F. You can cover with foil halfway through baking to protect the top from browning.
