Baked Tilapia

Description

This is a very simple baked tilapia recipe that is a quick and easy recipe that is an excellent source of protein! It is also a great alternative to salmon if you are on a budget.

Ingredients

  • 4 (4 oz) fillets tilapia
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon seafood seasoning, or to taste
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 (16 oz) package frozen cauliflower with broccoli and red pepper
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
